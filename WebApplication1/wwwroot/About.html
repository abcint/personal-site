I'm very excited yet concerned about the current state of softare and computing.

Security is starting to become more popular, and hopefully we can build upon this. What concerns me is that true security & stability measures almost always follows implementation.

Sure we have better security "built in", this idea makes developers assume their system is completely safe. Every new layer could be a vulnerability, and adds to our software environment (more complexity).

On top of this; we are increasing energy usage by adding more cores, off-loading to the cloud, and adding more "things" on the internet.
